Introduction

Many fractals are called self-similar (i.e. have a similar shape at arbitrary small scales) and are generated by repeating a construction pattern an infinite number of times. There is one of interest called the Sierpinski gasket (or Sierpinski triangle):

The Sierpinski gasket has very rich properties: like some fractals, the Sierpinski triangle is the attractor of the Chaos Game. Furthermore, its specific structure allows one to build analysis tools on the Sierpinski gasket (like the gradient, the Laplacian, for example). This website presents some known results in analysis on the Sierpinski gasket: the material contained on this website illustrates a Capstone class taught in Spring 2023 at Saint Louis University.
